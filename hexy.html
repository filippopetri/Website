Hexy

Hexapod Robot

Two degrees of freedom hexapod robot controlled by an Arduino UNO board, project realised for the A. Malignani graduation exam in the school year 2016/2017.

In the development of this thesis we addressed the design and creation of a six-legged robot controlled by an Arduino UNO board and equipped with 12 servomotors for movement, a Bluetooth module for remote control via smartphone app, a step down DC-DC converter for power supply, accelerometer and gyroscope.

Hexy is a hexapod robot, with six legs; each leg is moved by two digital servomotors controlled by the Arduino Uno microcontroller. This configuration is called a two-degree-of-freedom leg. To carry out the project, all the necessary plastic parts were designed using SolidWorks and 3D printed. The PCB board for the Arduino Uno was designed in order to optimize and simplify all the electrical connections.
