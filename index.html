About Me

My name is Filippo Petri, I'm an italian electronic engineering student at the University of Udine.

I graduated as electronic technician at the I.S.I.S. A. Malignani Udine and since then I fell in love with electronics. I also love photography, tinkering with software, mechanics, robotics, web design and fabricating things with 3D printers and CNC machines.

During the years of making things and thanks to my studies I developed skills in CAD design, PCB making, networking, web developing, Arduino and software engineering. I'm always interested in learning new stuff, making experience and improve my skills.
