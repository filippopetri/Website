About Me

My name is Filippo Petri, I'm an italian electronic engineer, graduated at the University of Udine.

I also graduated as electronic technician at the I.S.I.S. A. Malignani Udine. Since I was a child I enjoyed tinkering with electronics and mechanics. I also love photography, experimenting with software, mechanics, robotics, web design and fabricating things with 3D printers and CNC machines.

During the years I made many things and thanks to my experience in making small projects and with school and university courses I developed skills in CAD design, PCB making, networking, web developing, Arduino, software engineering and more. I'm always interested in learning new stuff, making experience and improve my skills.
