About Me

My name is Filippo Petri, I am an italian electronic engineer, graduated at the University of Udine.

I also graduated as electronic technician at the I.S.I.S. A. Malignani Udine. Ever since I was a child I enjoyed tinkering with electronics and mechanics. I also love photography, experimenting with software, mechanics, robotics, web design and fabricating things with 3D printers and CNC machines.

Thanks to the knowledge I acquired in school and university courses, together with the making of many personal projects, I developed skills in CAD design, PCB making, networking, web developing, Arduino, software engineering and more. I am always interested in learning new stuff, making experience and improving my skills.
